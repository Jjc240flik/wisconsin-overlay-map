Appendix Overview
The following maps are assembled from existing data at the time of the Brown
County Comprehensive Plan Update. The future land use data comes from each
community’s existing approved comprehensive plan, current as of March 2023.
The future land use map is approved as part of each community’s comprehensive plan and
is intended to guide planning and zoning decisions for that community. Brown County does
not enforce county-wide zoning, and all land use zoning decisions are the responsibility of
each individual municipality. Each community has approved and adopted its respective
comprehensive plan, and while Brown County may have assisted in that process, all the
future land use designations are determinedbyeach municipality. Future landuse mapsare
not the same as zoning maps and are not intended to enforce existing zoning requirements.
Each municipality is responsible for enforcing its zoning ordinance based on its approved
zoning map. For specific future land use or zoning questions, the municipality should be
contacteddirectly.
Every effort has been made to include the most future land use data, but any subsequent
updates made to any of these maps by the individual communities will not be updated in or
added to this document. This section is intended only to show a snapshot in time of each
community’s future land use map. Development trends and conditions change, and
communities may adjust their future land use map over time. These maps are not binding
and are advisory in nature. They are intended to help each municipality make land use and
zoning decisions based on their current adopted comprehensive plan at the time of this
document.
These maps are intended for general reference purposes only. The composite map of all
Brown County future land use plans is on the following page (Figure 2-12) and has
generalizedlanduse categories tosimplifythe bigpicture. Eachcommunitymayhave more
specific definitions of mixed-use commercial and residential, business/industrial parks, etc.
For acomplete pictureof a community,please refer tothatmunicipality’splan.(Note: Some
municipalities’ future land use data comes from the same dataset. In these instances, land
usecategoriesmayappearinthe legendthatdon’t appearinthat municipality).
