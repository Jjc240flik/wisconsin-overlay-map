9.b

Discuss/Consider/Act - Greenville Emergency Services Report
Review/Implementation
M - Greenville Emergency Services Report Review.pdf
Greenville Emergency Services Report .pdf
Audience members expressed concerns with current staffing and future full-time
staffing needs and also commented on report recommendations that are already
being implemented.
Jack Anderson recommended conducting an annual review of Fire Department
staff certifications to ensure records remain accurate and up to date, along with
implementing quarterly reporting to identify trends and potential areas for
improvement. He also requested that Fire/EMS staff gather additional
information regarding ambulance service operations from Grand Chute and
Kaukauna, including reviewing their documentation to help establish a foundation
for Greenville’s program. It was also noted that Adam Lenth has agreed to
assume the role of EMS Director.
