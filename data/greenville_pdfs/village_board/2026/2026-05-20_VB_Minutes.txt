Discussion of Fire Chief Position.
Emergency Service Report - Recommendation Fire Chief.pdf
Randy Immel, N1876 Donna Dr; explanation of the history of the public service
workers in Greenville, concerns about hiring a full-time Chief.
Mike Woods, W6669 Grandview Rd; concerns about full-time Chief.
Chris Schlechta, N1413 Star Dust Dr; suggested looking into daily call volume
and duties to determine the type of Chief that is needed.
Direction from Village Board is to make this a top priority by July 1st, 2026 to
gather the metrics of the Fire Station needs, form a committee if necessary, and
funnel Board questions to Chief to bring to fire committee. Brittany Helf is
looking for information on annual call volume of past 5-10 years, breakdown of
fire rescue service calls, active firefighter staffing levels and retention, challenges
with recruitment or open positions, understanding keeping up with training and
compliance requirements, estimate of administrative workload, fire inspections,
development reviews and prevention, compare that with population and service
demand projections, and compare our data compared to other similar sized
municipalities.
Summary report due July 1st, meeting July 8th.
