## 2024 Village Board / Ad Hoc Development Committee

9 meeting agendas downloaded (Jan-Oct 2024). These were **Ad Hoc Village Development Committee** meetings, not full Village Board meetings.

### Key Finding: Essler Property Concept Discussion

**March 12, 2024 — Ad Hoc Village Development Committee**

Agenda Item **3.a**: Discuss Subdivision Concept Plan

Supporting documents referenced:
- "55 Acres - Subdivision Mayflower Everglade.pdf"
- "Land to North - Pond-Park.pdf"

This is the **first documented discussion** of the Essler (Essler) property. The concept showed approximately 55 acres of developable land along Mayflower/Everglade — consistent with the current ~48-acre developable area.

The committee also discussed:
- Item 3.b: Possible Zoning Ordinance amendments
- Item 3.c: Discuss Possible Areas for Development
