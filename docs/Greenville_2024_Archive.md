## Key Finding: Essler Property Concept Discussion

**March 12, 2024 — Ad Hoc Village Development Committee** (clip_id=698)

Agenda Item **3.a**: Discuss Subdivision Concept Plan

Supporting documents referenced:
- "55 Acres - Subdivision Mayflower Everglade.pdf"
- "Land to North - Pond-Park.pdf"

This is the **only documented discussion** of the Essler concept in any 2024 meeting.
No PC meeting discussed it. No Village Board meeting discussed it.

### Timing of Events

| Date | Event |
|:----:|-------|
| Mar 12, 2024 | Ad Hoc Committee discusses Mayflower/Everglade concept plan |
| Jun 25, 2024 | Davel Engineering finalizes 78-lot concept plan for Nine Twenty Realty |
| ~Summer 2024 | Neighbor opposition; Sub-Area Plan C process begins |
| Jul 28, 2025 | Sub-Area C Final Plan approved by PC (7-0) |
| ~Late 2025 | Property listed on MLS at $1.28M |

**Who submitted the concept plan:** Nine Twenty Realty, through Davel Engineering. A separate entity from the current seller (Barbara/Estler family).
