# Lincoln County – Future Property Development Vision

> **Research status:** Source-backed draft. Needs future land use map extraction from County Comprehensive Plan.

## General Breakdown: Lincoln County’s Vision for Future Property Development

Based on Lincoln County’s comprehensive plan and zoning/land use resources, the county’s future development vision includes a 20-year growth framework, forest/farmland strategies, and a Future Land Use Map for participating county-zoned towns.

### 1. Growth Should Be Strategic, Not Scattered
Lincoln County’s comprehensive plan provides a framework for 20-year growth and should be used to test whether parcels are mapped for development transition.

### 2. Protect Agricultural / Forest Land
The county explicitly references strategies for forest, farmland, and related resources. Development screening must include forest/farmland preservation.

### 3. Concentrate Development Around Existing Urban Areas
Likely primary growth nodes to verify:
- Merrill
- Tomahawk
- Highway 51 corridor
- Towns participating in county comprehensive planning

### 4. Support Mixed-Use and Higher-Density Development
More intensive development is likely near Merrill, Tomahawk, and highway/service nodes.

### 5. Guide Growth Along Major Corridors
Key corridors to investigate:
- US Highway 51
- Highway 64
- Highway 86
- Highway 107

### 6. Each Municipality Controls Its Own Zoning
Important point: Lincoln County’s comprehensive plan includes a Future Land Use Map for participating towns, and rezone/comprehensive plan changes depend on that map.

### 7. Key Priorities for Future Development

| Priority | What Planners Want / What To Verify |
|---|---|
| Infrastructure Efficiency | Growth near Merrill, Tomahawk, and existing corridors |
| Agricultural / Forest Preservation | Check forest, farmland, and future land use map designations |
| Housing Variety | Verify planned residential areas in FLU map |
| Economic Growth | Identify commercial/industrial nodes near highways/service centers |
| Environmental Protection | Forest, wetlands, shoreland, and floodplain screening |

**Bottom Line**  
Lincoln County is a high-priority county for future land use map review because the county explicitly ties rezones and plan changes to its comprehensive plan and Future Land Use Map.

## Summary of Growth Patterns

**Highest Growth Areas:** Merrill, Tomahawk, and Highway 51 corridor.

**Moderate Growth Areas:** County-zoned towns mapped for future development.

**Low Growth / Agricultural Preservation Areas:** Forest/rural towns away from services and corridors.

**Built-Out Communities:** Merrill and Tomahawk should be checked for infill and planned edge growth.
