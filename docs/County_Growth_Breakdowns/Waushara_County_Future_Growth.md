# Waushara County – Future Property Development Vision

> **Research status:** Source-backed draft. Needs comprehensive plan / town-level plan extraction.

## General Breakdown: Waushara County’s Vision for Future Property Development

Based on Waushara County comprehensive plan resources, the county plan covers land use, development, and redevelopment guidance for public and private property.

### 1. Growth Should Be Strategic, Not Scattered
Growth should be evaluated through the county comprehensive plan, strategic plan, and town-level comprehensive plans.

### 2. Protect Agricultural Land
Waushara County has significant agricultural and rural land, especially in interior towns away from the lake corridor.

### 3. Concentrate Development Around Existing Urban Areas
Likely primary growth nodes to verify:
- Wautoma
- Berlin (partial)
- Redgranite
- Hancock
- Highway 21 / 73 / 22 corridor communities

### 4. Support Mixed-Use and Higher-Density Development
Higher-density uses are most likely near Wautoma and highway corridor nodes.

### 5. Guide Growth Along Major Corridors
Key corridors to investigate:
- State Highway 21
- State Highway 73
- State Highway 22

### 6. Each Municipality Controls Its Own Zoning
County comprehensive plan provides framework; town-level plans also apply.

### 7. Key Priorities for Future Development

| Priority | What Planners Want / What To Verify |
|---|---|
| Infrastructure Efficiency | Growth near Wautoma and corridor nodes |
| Agricultural Preservation | Town plans identify growth vs preservation areas |
| Housing Variety | Verify planned near communities |
| Economic Growth | Corridor and community-serving nodes |
| Environmental Protection | Lake region, wetlands, and rural landscapes |

**Bottom Line**  
Waushara County should be researched as a corridor/rural county with best targets near Wautoma and Highway 21 corridor.

## Summary of Growth Patterns

**Highest Growth Areas:** Wautoma, Highway 21 corridor.

**Low Growth / Agricultural Preservation Areas:** Rural agricultural/lake towns.

**Built-Out Communities:** Wautoma should be checked for infill.
