# Juneau County – Future Property Development Vision

> **Research status:** Source-backed draft. Needs official comp plan / future land use map extraction.

## General Breakdown: Juneau County’s Vision for Future Property Development

Juneau County future growth research should be grounded in county and local comprehensive plans, GIS land information resources, and town-level zoning/future land use maps.

### 1. Growth Should Be Strategic, Not Scattered
Future development should be focused near existing cities, villages, utilities, and transportation corridors rather than scattered rural conversion.

### 2. Protect Agricultural Land
A key research question is where agricultural land is intended to remain agricultural versus where future land use maps show transition to residential or commercial use.

### 3. Concentrate Development Around Existing Urban Areas
Likely primary growth nodes to verify:
- Mauston
- New Lisbon
- Elroy
- Necedah
- Camp Douglas / I-90/94 corridor areas

### 4. Support Mixed-Use and Higher-Density Development
Any higher-intensity development should be checked near serviced communities and highway access points.

### 5. Guide Growth Along Major Corridors
Key corridors to investigate:
- I-90/94
- State Highway 82
- State Highway 58
- US Highway 12/16

### 6. Each Municipality Controls Its Own Zoning
County and local plans must be checked together. Town comprehensive plans may control or inform land-use directions more specifically than county summaries.

### 7. Key Priorities for Future Development

| Priority | What Planners Want / What To Verify |
|---|---|
| Infrastructure Efficiency | Growth near Mauston/New Lisbon and service nodes |
| Agricultural Preservation | Protect rural/agricultural land unless mapped for transition |
| Housing Variety | Verify future residential areas in town/city plans |
| Economic Growth | Corridor sites near I-90/94 and service centers |
| Environmental Protection | Wetlands, floodplain, and recreation/natural areas need screening |

**Bottom Line**  
Juneau County should be researched around Mauston/New Lisbon and I-90/94 corridor opportunities first, while rural/agricultural areas require strong future land use confirmation before considering development.

## Summary of Growth Patterns

**Highest Growth Areas:** Mauston, New Lisbon, and I-90/94 corridor nodes.

**Moderate Growth Areas:** Elroy, Necedah, Camp Douglas.

**Low Growth / Agricultural Preservation Areas:** Rural interior towns away from services/corridors.

**Built-Out Communities:** Mauston should be reviewed for redevelopment/infill plus planned edge growth.
