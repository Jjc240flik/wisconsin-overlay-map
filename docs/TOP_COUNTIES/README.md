## Methodology
All ratings follow the **Cody Bjugan / North Star framework**:
- **High**: Villages and towns directly outside city limits with FLU showing residential expansion
- **Moderate to High**: Edge-adjacent communities with some development pressure
- **Moderate**: Secondary rings, cities, or localized growth centers
- **Low to Moderate / Low**: Rural villages and towns far from metro areas

**Key Principle:** Surrounding cities will always be villages. The highest potential is in the directly adjacent municipalities.
